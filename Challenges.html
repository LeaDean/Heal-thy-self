Lessons from conflict

People often say, if it had not been for tough challenges that they have gone through in the past they would not be the person that they have become, this implies that there is potential for valuable growth and learning in all our challenges. These lessons learned through tough challenges are obvious in hindsight but is it possible to be aware of this process during the moment and be able to more fully able to increase our level of learning while meeting our challenges head on?
